Frequently Asked Questions

Where are the nodes located?

The current nodes used are located in Europe, & United Kingdom on servers for LimeNodes.

How many nodes are there?

We currently have eu01, & uk01, We do plan on adding more soon.

Would there be much downtime?

Here at LimeNodes keeping uptime and quality is our priority, so there should be little to no downtime of nodes. If there ever is it would be very rare and chances are that it wouldn't be our fault e.g. a power cut, but sometimes we will do maintenance and post warnings before this.

How do I make and access my servers?

When you first create an account at our dashboard, an account will also be created for our panel, the site where you monitor and control your servers, to make more servers you would create them on the dashboard again.

You can make a ticket in the discord and a staff member will help you out.
