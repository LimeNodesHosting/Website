Partner Information

Last updated: 1/17/2023

This page talks about what you are required to do as a partner

How can I become a partner?

If you decide you want to become a partner then you will have to fill out the form below. This can be either a Discord server or a YouTube channel but we can consider requests as long as they are reasonable.

What do I get from being a partner?

If you are accepted and become a partner you will be given at the moment infinite credits to host your servers and access to post in the partners servers channel and promote your discord. This system is set to change in the next coming weeks.

What are the requirements?

Discord Members: 250. YouTube Subscribers: 750
