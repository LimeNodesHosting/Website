Privacy Policy

Last updated: 12/21/2022

This privacy policy explains how LimeNodes ("Services", "Websites") uses the data we collect from you when you use our Service.

What data do we collect?

Summary: We store user and server data.

LimeNodes collects the following data:

  • Email
  • Password (Only for dashboard data, we cannot see the password)
  • Sessions (To keep you signed in)
  • Server configuration
  • Server data (i.e. worlds)

How do we collect your data?

Summary: We store data given by you to us.

You directly provide data we collect, i.e. by signing up. We collect data and process data when you use the Service.

How do we use your data?

Summary: We use your data to provide the Service.

LimeNodes collects your data, so we can provide the Service to you. We have never, do not, and will never sell your data.

How do we store your data?

Summary: We store your data in a secure database.

LimeNodes stores your data in a secure database instance in Germany. We will inform you of any data breaches within 48h of us being alerted about them.

What log and analytic data do we collect?

Summary: We store data your browser sends to us when you visit the website.

Like most websites, LimeNodes collects information that your browser sends whenever you visit the website. This data may include your internet protocol (IP) address, browser type, date and time stamp, referring/exit pages, actions, endpoints, status codes (responses) and time spent on each page. This data does not contain any personally identifiable information. We use this information for analyzing trends, administering the site, tracking users' movement on the website, and gathering demographic information. In our case, we use a self-hosted Plausible Analytics as this service.

What are Cookies?

Summary: Cookies are text files on your computer.

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information. When you visit our Website, we may collect information from you automatically through cookies or similar technology.

How do we use cookies?

Summary: We use cookies in various ways.

LimeNodes uses cookies in a range of ways to improve your experience on our Website, i.e. keeping you signed in.

How to manage cookies

Summary: You can disable cookies, but the Website may not function.

You can set your browser not to accept cookies. However, in a few cases, some of our Website features may behave unexpectedly or fail to function as a result.

Privacy policies of other websites

Summary: If you click a link, you should read their privacy policy.

LimeNodes contains links to other external websites. Our privacy policy only applies to our Websites, so if you click on a link to another website, you should read their privacy policy.

Changes to our privacy policy

Summary: We can change our privacy policy at any time without notice.

LimeNodes keeps its privacy policy under regular review and places any updates on this web page. The LimeNodes privacy policy may be subject to change at any given time without notice. You can view the last updated date at the top of the page.

How to contact us

Summary: Email us.

For any clarifications about LimeNodes' privacy policy, the data we hold on you, or you would like to exercise one of your data protection rights, please do not hesitate to contact us at support@limenodes.com.
